Diary Study

I am currently working in a Diary study assignment. Diary study, I utilized the time tracker web application to follow my study habits conduct for one week, to have a superior comprehension of my study patterns. By tracking and recording the times I was studying, I was able to perceive how the time of the day and the day of the week affect my study outlines. To record when I was studying, I just tapped the Start Time button to record the present time stamp and to begin a timer in the background. Once, I was finished studying I tapped the Stop Time button to stop the timer and to record the present timestamp. Each time I finished a studying session, I had the option to perceive to what extent the session was and what date it was on.
